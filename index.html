About Caleb Wallace

I am a sophomore at Bob Jones University studying communications. I work for the Department of Public Safety here, on campus. I attend Palmetto Baptist Church in Piedmont SC. 

During the summer, at my home church, I write and act out skits for the kids at vacation bible school. I usually write the script with a friend of mine which is a lot of fun but what is more fun is creating the character and creating their look. Overall, the best part is always watching the kids react to the character every night they attend VBS.

I enjoy listening to music and critiquing movies with my brother and my best friend.
